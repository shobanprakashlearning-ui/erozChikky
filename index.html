Preserving original culinary methods.

What started in our grandmother's kitchen in Madurai has now become a mission to bring authentic Kadalai Mittai back to the modern palate. We noticed that mass-produced chikkis had lost that distinct roasted aroma and were mostly made with liquid glucose.

At Chikki & Co., we went back to the roots. We hand-roast our peanuts over perfect heat, melt unadulterated jaggery to the exact string-consistency, and pour it onto wooden blocks just like it was done decades ago.

Every bite you take isn't just a snack; it's a piece of our heritage.
